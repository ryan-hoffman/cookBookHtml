Biscuits

2 c flour

2 t baking soda

1 t baking powder

1 t salt

6 T butter, cold

1/2 c buttermilk or whey


  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Cut the cold butter into small cubes and "cut into" the flour mixture.
  4. When the mixture resembles coarse cornmeal, add the buttermilk or whey and mix until the ingredients come together
  5. Turn out onto a floured surface and shape into a log, about an inch and a half thick.
  6. Cut into 12 equal sized discs and space evenly on a sheet pan.
  7. Bake for 15 to 18 minutes, or until golden brown.
